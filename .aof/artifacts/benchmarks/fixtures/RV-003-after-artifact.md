# AOF For AI Product Teams

AOF helps an AI product team answer one hard question reliably:

**Can we prove that our AI workflow is improving, not just generating more output?**

## What Buyers Get

- a runtime that records task, review, and decision evidence
- a review loop that can reopen weak work instead of auto-praising it
- a benchmark trail that shows whether quality actually improved

## Why This Version Is More Credible

- it names the operator problem in plain language
- it points to benchmark evidence instead of self-assertion
- it explains what changed in the review loop, not only what AOF claims in theory

## Evidence

- runtime-loop proofs show end-to-end orchestration traces
- review-validity benchmarks show how weak artifacts are blocked
- runtime-discipline benchmarks show when missing runtime evidence becomes a failure

## What A Buyer Should Understand On First Read

AOF is not selling "AI magic."  
It is selling a way to make AI work inspectable, reviewable, and harder to over-score by accident.

## Next Step

Use AOF when the team needs evidence that an AI process improved after critique, not just that it produced another draft.
